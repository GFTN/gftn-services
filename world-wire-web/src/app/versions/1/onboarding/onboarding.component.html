Onboarding

So how much time and effort will it take to get up and running on World Wire? Typically, we believe 1-2 months is a safe estimate, but that may vary based on a variety of factors. In order to accurately estimate the timing and effort required, the following are a few key factors that your organization should take into consideration:

  • Desired role and capabilities on World Wire
  • Team size and availability
  • Complexity of existing back-end systems
  • Current understanding of World Wire concepts
